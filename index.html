Offered Courses
Bachelor of Science in
Computer Science
The Bachelor of Science in Computer Studies program offers a comprehensive exploration of computer science fundamentals, equipping students with a strong foundation in programming, algorithms, and computational thinking. Through hands-on projects and theoretical coursework, graduates emerge adept at solving complex problems, designing innovative solutions, and contributing effectively to the ever-evolving field of technology.
Bachelor of Science in
Information Technology
The Bachelor of Science in Information Technology program equips students with the essential skills and knowledge to navigate the rapidly evolving world of technology, preparing them for careers in software development, cybersecurity, data analytics, and more. Through a blend of theoretical foundations and hands-on practical experience, graduates emerge ready to tackle the challenges of the digital age with confidence and expertise.
Offered Majors
  • Information Technology
  • Computer Science
SMP
Service Management Program
This major focuses on coordinating and optimizing IT services to meet business objectives through efficient resource management, strategic planning, and continual improvement processes.
WMAD
Web and Mobile Development
This major delves into the creation and optimization of dynamic and user-friendly applications and websites for both web and mobile platforms, utilizing various programming languages and frameworks.
NA
Network Administration
This major involves managing and maintaining computer networks, security, and performance through the configuration, monitoring, and troubleshooting of network devices and services.
IS
Intelligent System
This major involves the development and implementation of advanced algorithms and technologies to enable machines to simulate human-like intelligence, solve complex problems, and adapt to changing environments autonomously.
